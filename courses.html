Metalabs Course Program

About Metalabs Courses

Metalabs at Telkom University is a research laboratory specializing in interactive multimedia, game development, game design, and monetization. Guided by its core values of Education, Incubation, and Monetization, Metalabs equips researchers and students with knowledge, fosters innovation to transform ideas into real-world applications, and ensures projects generate measurable economic and societal value.

This page offers short course materials designed for institutions such as:

  • King Mongkut's University of Technology Thonburi (KMUTT) in Thailand
  • Management & Science University (MSU), Malaysia
  • Universiti Kuala Lumpur (UniKL), Malaysia
  • Indonesian Cyber Education (ICE) Institute
  • Vocational School Graduate Academy (VSGA) for interactive multimedia

By combining academic rigor with practical solutions, Metalabs drives impactful research and collaboration, turning creativity into meaningful outcomes for education, industry, and the wider community.
