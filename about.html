About Metalabs

Interactive Multimedia For Everything

Who We Are

Metalabs is a research laboratory at Telkom University specializing in interactive multimedia for everything. With a mission to innovate and inspire, Metalabs supports research and community service programs, contributing to the university's commitment to impactful knowledge development and dissemination.

The lab is guided by three core values: Education, Incubation, and Monetization. These principles shape our approach to research, ensuring that projects are not only academically rigorous but also create practical solutions and sustainable value. By integrating these values, Metalabs helps researchers and students transform ideas into impactful outcomes.
